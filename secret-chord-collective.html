Secret Chord
Collective Nathan Vanderpool · Berlin

A network of musicians playing songs written and arranged by Nathan Vanderpool. These are songs that needed more than one voice to exist — arranged, layered, unafraid of difficulty. The collective has worked with Ronja Polzin, the Agapic Chants project, and others who bring their own register to the same underlying impulse.
