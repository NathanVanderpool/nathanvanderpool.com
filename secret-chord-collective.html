Berlin · Est. 2020

A network of musicians playing songs written and arranged by Nathan Vanderpool.

These are songs that needed more than one voice to exist. Secret Chord Collective is where the music gets complicated — arranged, layered, darker. Collaborators have included Ronja Polzin and the Agapic Chants project, each bringing their own register to the same underlying impulse.
